We're excited to officially introduce **Unsupervised**.

We are a physical AI company founded by world-class researchers and engineers with decades of combined experience in robotics. Our mission is simple: make automation flexible, accessible, and genuinely intelligent.

Manufacturing is one of the most important industries in the world — and one of the most underserved by modern technology. We're here to change that.

## Who We Are

Our team has spent years at the frontier of robotics research and real-world deployment. We've built systems, broken them, iterated, and shipped. We know what it takes to make robots work reliably outside of a lab — and we've built Unsupervised to do exactly that.

## What We're Building

We're building physical AI that works across any robot hardware — arms, mobile platforms, and beyond — and can be deployed quickly into real manufacturing environments.

Our focus right now is on precision assembly and inspection tasks, but our platform is designed to grow with your operations.

## What's Next

We are actively working with our first customers and partners. If you're in manufacturing and want to explore what AI-powered automation could mean for your operations, [reach out](/contact).

More updates coming soon.

*— Glen Berseth, Founder*
